AQLI Factsheet Dashboard

The AQLI Factsheet Dashboard provides a clear and accessible summary of air pollution and life expectancy impacts across countries, regions, and cities. It presents high-level insights, trends over time, and comparisons against WHO and national standards.

This dashboard is designed to support policymakers, researchers, journalists, and the public in understanding air quality challenges and the potential benefits of cleaner air.
